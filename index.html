Hello, we are Monash Calisthenics

We're the student-led calisthenics community at Monash University, embracing enthusiasts of all levels. Driven by our shared enthusiasm, we come together to collectively practice and refine calisthenics, nurturing not only our physical abilities but also cultivating a closely connected community of individuals who share the same mindset.
